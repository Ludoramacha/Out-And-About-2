Budget-Friendly Hotspots for Uni Students

Food Hotspots

Pizza Place - Student Discount

Grab a delicious and filling pizza slice for under $5! They offer a 10% discount with a valid student ID.

Price: $3 - $8

Tip: Ask about their daily specials for even better deals.

Cafe Student - Affordable Meals

Their daily pasta special for just $6 is a student favorite. A cozy spot perfect for a quick lunch or studying.

Price: $5 - $10

Tip: Bring your own reusable cup for a $0.50 discount on coffee.

Entertainment Hotspots

Campus Movie Night - Free!

Every Friday night, the student union hosts a free movie screening in the auditorium. Bring your own snacks!

The Local Cinema - Student Discount

Show your student ID for a discounted ticket price on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Price: $7 (with student ID)

Nature Hotspots

University Park - Free Access

A beautiful green space right on campus. Perfect for picnics, studying outdoors, or just relaxing.

Riverside Trail - Walking & Biking

Enjoy a scenic walk or bike ride along the Riverside Trail. It's easily accessible by public transport.

Tip: Bring water and wear comfortable shoes!
